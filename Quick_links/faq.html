Frequently Asked Questions

What is Urban Spoon?
Urban Spoon is a restaurant management system that helps you order food online and manage reservations.
How do I place an order?
You can browse the menu, select your dishes, and place an order through our website or mobile app.
Can I cancel my reservation?
Yes, you can cancel your reservation from your account dashboard up to 24 hours before your booking time.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and digital wallets for secure and easy payments.
Do you offer home delivery?
Yes, we provide home delivery for all online orders. Delivery charges may apply based on location.
How do I contact customer support?
You can reach our customer support via email at support@urbanspoon.com or call us at +123 456 7890.
Can I track my order?
Yes, you can track your order in the "My Orders" section after logging into your account.
Is there a loyalty program?
Yes! We offer a loyalty program where you earn points for each order and redeem them later.
How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.
Do you support group bookings?
Yes, for large groups and events, contact our support team for special arrangements.
Are there any discounts for first-time users?
Yes, new users can apply promo codes during checkout for special discounts.
Can I change delivery address after placing order?
Yes, but only within 5 minutes of placing the order. Contact support immediately for help.
