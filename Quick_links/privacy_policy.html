Privacy Policy

At Urban Spoon, we are committed to maintaining your trust by protecting your personal information. This policy outlines the data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding it.

Collection of Personal Information

We only collect personal details when you choose to provide them. Common instances include:

  • Creating or updating your user profile
  • Participating in surveys or offers
  • Communicating with customer support
  • Making online orders or payments

All personal data is collected with your consent and solely for service improvement purposes.

Sharing with Third Parties

We respect your privacy. Urban Spoon does not trade or sell your information. Limited data may be shared only with verified partners who assist in:

  • Site performance and analytics
  • Customer care services
  • Order packaging and delivery

All partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Security Measures

Your data security is our top priority. We employ the latest measures including:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption on all transactions
  • Secure, encrypted storage of sensitive details
  • Regular vulnerability assessments and system updates

While no method is 100% secure, we strive to protect your data using best industry standards.
